WHAT IS LEADERBOARD?

Leaderboard is a board showing the rank of leaders in our referral program. It is created to help all our members to grow in wealth and become more financially stable. As we are ready to be paying you monthly commission based on our leaderboard policy.

How does the leaderboard works?

You will be paid a minimum of ₦5,000 and maximum of ₦100,000 every month once you refer atleast 10 subscribed members within the duration of each month.
This does not affect the 10% commission you will earn when they subscribe and also the commission you will earn whenever they made a transaction.

How much can I make within a month?

You can make up to ₦100,000 within a month.

  • If you refer 10 Basic subscribers, you will earn ₦500 on each subscriber which the total is ₦5,000.
  • If you refer 10 Value subscribers, you will earn ₦1,000 on each subscriber which the total is ₦10000.
  • If you refer 10 Premium subscribers, you will earn ₦1,500 on each subscriber which the total is ₦15,000.
  • If you refer 10 Diamond subscribers, you will earn ₦2,000 on each subscriber which the total is ₦20,000.
  • If you refer 10 Gold subscribers, you will earn ₦2,500 on each subscriber which the total is ₦25,000.
  • And also if you can refer up to 20 Gold subscribers, you will earn ₦100,000 in total.

How do I get paid?

Once you have referred up to 10 subscribed members, your money will be added to your commission in your dashboard which you can withdraw to your bank account or use it to purchase any product.

How do I see my leaderboard earnings?

You can see it in your dashboard. Log into your account, click on MENU, then click on My Leaderboard.

Who is qualified in the leaderboard program?

Only subscribed members are qualified in the leaderboard program. Free members are not qualified.

How many subscriber must I refer within a month?

You must refer atleast 10 subscribed members within a month before you can get paid for the leaderboard.

What if I'm unable to refer up to 10 subscribers within a month?

If your subscribed referrals are not up-to 10 before the month ends, all the learderboard money earned from those that you referred in that particular month will be automatically converted into Point Value(PV). And the conversion will be 10% of the amount earned. So if you earn ₦3,000 in your leaderboard within a month and you are unable to refer up-to 10 Subscribed Members, the ₦3,000 will be converted to 300PV which will be added to your Point Value.

Will I be paid if my referrals are Free Members?

No. You will only be paid if your referrals are Subscribed members.

When will I be paid?

Our payment for leaderboard is 12A.M on the 1st of each month.
